HISTORY OF NATIONAL PARKS IN ENGLAND AND WALES

The establishment of national parks within England and Wales over time expresses the historical relationship between conservation and national heritage in these nations. This is best expressed in the Environment Act 1995, which asserts this relationship as: to conserve and enhance the natural beauty, wildlife and cultural heritage of the National Parks and to promote opportunities for the understanding and enjoyment of the special qualities of the National Parks by the public.
